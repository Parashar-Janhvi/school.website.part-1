About Us

St. Paul's Church College is a school in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established on 25 January 1980 by the Church of North India and is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations board of schools. The principal of the school is Mr. Vivek Daniels (2009-present) who replaced Mrs. Veronica Carville (1980-2009).
